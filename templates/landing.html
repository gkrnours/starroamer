Star Roamer aim to give you better route through the star and accurate time of arrival.

How better? It depend of what you are flying and where you are flying it. The idea is to take into account that each solar system is unique.
Some bright, other dark.
Some busy, other empty.
Some huge, other tiny.

Do you know how huge these can get ? How long can be the trip between two stargate ? If you have experienced the loneliness of piloting a freighter inside on of these behemoth, you might understand how a detour using small system can be a better route.
